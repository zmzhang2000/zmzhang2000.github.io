Biography

I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), with an expected graduation in the summer of 2026. My supervisors are Prof. Houqiang Li and Prof. Wengang Zhou. I received the B.E. degree from Shandong University (SDU) in 2021, where I was advised by Prof. Liqiang Nie and Prof. Xuemeng Song.

My research interests include large language models and reinforcement learning. I am presently working on large reasoning models for code generation.

I am currently working as an intern in Qwen Team, Alibaba.

I am actively seeking job opportunities, and if you are interested in me, feel free to reach out to me via email.
